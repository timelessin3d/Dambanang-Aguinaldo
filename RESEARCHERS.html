Researchers

The Dambanang Aguinaldo: A Digital Heritage Experience is a capstone project developed to preserve cultural heritage through a 3D interactive web platform combining research, design, and development.

Project Contributions

The project was developed through a collaborative effort involving historical research, website development, 3D modeling, and user interface design. Each member contributed to different aspects of the system including content development, visual design, technical implementation, and integration of the virtual tour. The combined work resulted in an interactive digital heritage platform that presents Dambanang Aguinaldo in an immersive and educational experience.
